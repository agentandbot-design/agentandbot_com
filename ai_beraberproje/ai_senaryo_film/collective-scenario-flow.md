# Collective Scenario Flow

ai_senaryo is the collective AI story, scenario and video creation workspace.
It can run independently, but LesTupid apps may launch it from context.

## Static Objects

These are stable pages:

- scenario project;
- story bible;
- character/persona page;
- school/campus/event page;
- prompt library;
- render pipeline;
- rights and contributor ledger.

## Flow Events

These move through the feed:

- idea submission;
- prompt draft;
- scene rewrite;
- storyboard card;
- AI render request;
- review comment;
- contributor vote;
- consent/credit update;
- proof or export event.

## LesTupid Entry Points

- Les Go: campus/event/library/course check-in can launch a story or service
  simulation board.
- Les Poke: quests can become scripts, creator challenges or location stories.
- Les Wait: queue problems can become service simulations before engineering.
- Les Match: safety scenarios can test how matching prompts should behave.
- Les Certification: reviewers can train on generated evidence scenarios.
- Les Commerce: products, venues and student offers can become honest stories.

## Collaboration Rules

- AI-generated scenes must be labeled.
- Human contributors must choose visible credit, pseudonym or anonymous mode.
- Real minors cannot be used as characters without a dedicated minor-safe
  policy and guardian/school approval where required.
- Real places can be referenced as public venues, but private details are not
  imported by default.
- Render metadata should include prompt, source, model, reviewer and export
  status.

## First MVP Board

Columns:

- `Idea`
- `Prompt`
- `Scene`
- `Storyboard`
- `Render`
- `Review`
- `Export`

Each card carries:

- source app;
- contributor mode;
- AI/human label;
- rights status;
- tempo: live, short, today, ongoing or stable.
