# KADRO LesTupid Policy

KADRO is the AI worker/persona roster used by AgentAndBot and optionally exposed
inside LesTupid products.

## Identity Classes

- `lestupid_owned_agent`: LesTupid-created AI worker.
- `external_owned_agent`: user, school, venue, merchant or organization-owned
  AI worker.
- `human_operator`: a real person operating or supervising an agent.
- `fictional_persona`: creative persona for scenario/story/video work.

Every visible KADRO entity must show its identity class. Fictional and AI
entities must never be presented as real human students, mentors or employees.

## Where KADRO Appears

- LesTupid Go: agent help cards for study, forms, sponsor prep, venue support
  and scenario launch.
- Les Match: labeled AI mentor/worker/task suggestions only; no unlabeled human
  matching.
- Les Wait: queue explanation, staff note drafts, venue demand summaries.
- Les Poke: quest drafting, location story prompts, creator challenge ideas.
- Les Certification: evidence summary and review draft only.
- Les Commerce: merchant support, offer clarity, product/story assistant.
- ai_senaryo: actor, writer, editor, reviewer, storyboard assistant.

## Consent Rules

- Private data is opt-in per task.
- Agents cannot read private messages, location trails, payment details or
  school records by default.
- Agent-to-human introductions require Les Match consent when people are
  involved.
- Agent-to-task routing can happen without exposing a person profile.

## Operating Rules

- No fake social account warming, deceptive bot behavior or platform evasion.
- No silent posting, messaging, purchasing, enrollment or certification.
- No final decisions for certification, matchmaking, payment or student safety.
- Every agent task should have owner, scope, data boundary and audit metadata.

## Good First LesTupid Roles

- Study worker: turns a course/check-in into a plan or quiz.
- Sponsor prep worker: drafts sponsor/mentor notes for students.
- Venue ops worker: summarizes wait/queue complaints for places.
- Certification reviewer: drafts evidence summaries for human review.
- Scenario worker: helps teams write a campus story, film scene or quest.
